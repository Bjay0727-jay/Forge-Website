- Tone: Confident, warm, authoritative
- Pace: Moderate (not rushed, not slow)
- Style: Conversational but professional

### Music Recommendations:
- Style: Modern corporate, tech-forward
- Tempo: 100-120 BPM
- Mood: Optimistic, innovative
- Sources: Epidemic Sound, Artlist, Musicbed

- Resolution: 1920x1080 (minimum)
- Frame rate: 30fps (60fps for smooth cursor)
- Cursor: Highlighted with click indicators

- Secondary: #1E293B (Slate)
- Accent: #06B6D4 (Cyan)
- Background: #0F172A (Dark slate)

- Format: MP4 (H.264)
- Resolution: 1920x1080
- Bitrate: 10-15 Mbps
- Audio: AAC, 48kHz
