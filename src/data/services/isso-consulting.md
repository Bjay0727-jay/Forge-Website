## Professional ISSO Services

Finding and retaining qualified Information System Security Officers is one of the biggest challenges facing federal agencies and contractors. Forge Cyber Defense provides experienced, cleared ISSOs who integrate seamlessly with your team.

### Engagement Models

- **Full-Time Embedded** — Dedicated ISSO working as part of your team, managing all security operations for assigned systems
- **Part-Time Advisory** — Senior ISSO support for organizations with multiple systems needing oversight
- **Project-Based** — Focused engagements for ATO preparation, assessment support, or compliance remediation

### What Our ISSOs Deliver

Our ISSOs go beyond basic compliance checkbox activities. They provide:

- Proactive vulnerability management and risk mitigation
- Continuous monitoring deliverable preparation and submission
- POA&M management with remediation tracking
- Security architecture review and recommendations
- Incident detection, response, and reporting
- Authorizing Official and auditor liaison

### Qualifications

All Forge Cyber Defense ISSOs hold relevant certifications (CISSP, CISM, CAP, Security+) and have direct experience with federal agency environments. Many hold active security clearances and understand the unique requirements of classified and sensitive environments.
